Our Services

Professional paintless dent repair services with 30+ years of expertise

Hail Damage Repair

Professional hail damage restoration without repainting. We work with insurance companies to provide accurate estimates while maintaining your vehicle's factory finish and warranty.

Door Ding Removal

Quick, same-day fixes for those annoying parking lot dings and minor dents. Most repairs completed within hours, restoring your vehicle to its original condition.

Crease & Panel Restoration

Advanced crease removal and complex panel restoration using specialized tools and techniques. We handle challenging repairs that others can't.

Mobile PDR Service

Fully-equipped mobile van service — we come to your home, office, or any convenient location. Professional repairs performed on-site with the same quality as our shop.

About Dents Master

For over 30 years, Dents Master Franchise has been Central Florida's premier destination for paintless dent repair services and professional PDR training. Our commitment to precision, quality, and excellence has earned us the trust of thousands of satisfied customers and trained hundreds of successful PDR technicians.

Located in Winter Haven, we serve the entire Central Florida region with both in-shop services and mobile repairs. Our mission is to provide unmatched PDR services while sharing our expertise through comprehensive training programs that maintain the highest industry standards.
